## California State Universities (CSU) {#csu}

CSU campuses provide substantial benefits including free or reduced transit passes, food pantries, emergency housing, fitness centers, and mental health services.

**Featured:**
- CSU East Bay - Free AC Transit EasyPass, emergency housing support
- San Francisco State University - Gator Groceries food pantry, free Adobe software
- San Jose State University - Spartan Food Pantry, 128,000 sq ft recreation center
- Sonoma State University - Lobo's Pantry, emergency housing

## University of California (UC) {#uc}

UC campuses offer comprehensive student support including free/reduced transit (BayPass/GatorPass), food access, emergency funding, recreation facilities, and mental health services. Many programs require no financial documentation.

**Featured:**
- UC Berkeley - Free AC Transit pass, BayPass, food pantry, CHAOS gear rentals
- UC San Francisco - BayPass, late-night Lyft support (up to $150/month), food market
- UC College of the Law, San Francisco - Free legal clinics (open to community), merit scholarships

## Private Universities {#private}

Private universities serve Bay Area students at Stanford, USF, Santa Clara, and others. Many offer comparable benefits to UC/CSU including food access, transit support, fitness centers, and emergency assistance.

**Featured:**
- Stanford University - Extensive recreation facilities, equipment rentals, free counseling
- UC San Francisco Law School - Free legal clinics (open to community), guaranteed housing
- Santa Clara University - Bronco Food Pantry (trust-based), free fitness center
- University of San Francisco - Unlimited Muni pass, food pantry

## SparkPoint Centers

[SparkPoint Centers by United Ways Bay Area](https://uwba.org/what-we-do/sparkpoint-program/) provide free, confidential, judgment-free financial coaching and connections to basic needs support. Services include:

- **Financial stability:** Coaching, budgeting, credit repair, tax help
- **Employment & education:** Job search, skills training, career growth
- **Food & housing security:** Access to food, rental support, transit benefits
- **Long-term goals:** Savings, income growth, debt reduction
