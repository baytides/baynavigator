Bay Navigator helps residents discover financial assistance programs available throughout the San Francisco Bay Area. Whether you're looking to reduce utility bills, save on transit, access affordable food, or find discounts on internet service, this app connects you with programs you may qualify for.

PROGRAM SOURCES

All program information is compiled from publicly available websites. Each program listing links directly to its official source. Sources include:

• California Public Utilities Commission (cpuc.ca.gov) - LifeLine, Medical Baseline
• California Dept. of Social Services (cdss.ca.gov) - CalFresh, CalWorks
• California Dept. of Community Services (csd.ca.gov) - LIHEAP, Weatherization
• California Dept. of Health Care Services (dhcs.ca.gov) - Medi-Cal, Dental
• California DMV (dmv.ca.gov) - Vehicle programs
• California State Parks (parks.ca.gov) - Park passes

• IRS (irs.gov) - VITA tax preparation

KEY FEATURES
• Browse programs by category, county, or search
• View eligibility requirements before applying
• Direct links to official program websites
• No account or personal information required

WHO THIS IS FOR
Bay Navigator serves income-eligible individuals and families, seniors, people with disabilities, veterans, and anyone experiencing financial hardship.
